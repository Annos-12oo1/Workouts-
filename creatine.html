Creatine Supplement

Creatine is one of the most researched and effective supplements for improving performance in high-intensity training and increasing muscle mass.

What is Creatine?

Creatine is a naturally occurring compound found in muscle cells. It helps produce ATP (adenosine triphosphate), which provides energy for muscle contractions.

Benefits

  • Boosts strength and power output
  • Improves high-intensity exercise performance
  • Supports muscle growth and recovery
  • May enhance brain function and reduce fatigue

How to Use

The common method is to take 3–5 grams daily. Some choose to load with 20g/day for 5–7 days, then reduce to 3–5g/day as a maintenance dose.

Is It Safe?

Yes, creatine is safe for long-term use and is backed by decades of research. Drink plenty of water when supplementing.
