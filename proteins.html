Proteins

Protein supplements are essential for muscle recovery, muscle gain, and maintaining overall health for active individuals. They provide the body with the building blocks needed for muscle tissue repair and growth.

Types of Protein Supplements

  • Whey Protein: Fast-digesting, ideal post-workout.
  • Casein Protein: Slow-digesting, ideal before sleep.
  • Plant-Based Proteins: Good for vegans (pea, rice, soy, etc.).
  • Isolate & Hydrolysate: More refined and fast-absorbing forms.

When to Take Protein

  • Post-workout (30 minutes after training)
  • In the morning (to break overnight fasting)
  • Before bed (especially casein protein)

Benefits

  • Faster muscle recovery
  • Increased lean muscle mass
  • Supports weight loss by reducing appetite
